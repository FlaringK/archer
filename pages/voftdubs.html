VOFT Dubs is a group of voice actors under the umbrella of Time Archer Studios and licensed under the FRAFworks Collective. With 10 years of experience dubbing smaller fan content for Homestuck, as well as quite a few other fandoms, our current goal is to provide Let’s Reads for every piece of official Homestuck media (as well as a good handful of FRAF media). From the Epilogues, to Hiveswap, to Paradox Space, we plan to provide a dub for each and every one!
