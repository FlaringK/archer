Project Soundpage (otherwise known as Project [S]) is an animation project led by Time Archer studios and licensed by the FRAFworks collective with one simple goal: animate the entirety of homestuck (or as much as we can handle before we drop dead).

Sounds easy enough, right?

We know it’s a massive undertaking, but we’re here because we all love this story and what it has to say. What was once an unorganized dream of dozens of fans has become something real and tangible-- a team that’s devoted to help share this story in a medium we love. We hope you’re just as excited as we are!
